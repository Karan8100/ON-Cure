Common Symptoms of Brain Tumor

Chronic Headaches

Persistent and worsening headaches that do not respond to usual treatment.

Blurred Vision

Double or blurry vision can be a sign of pressure on the brain.

Seizures

Sudden, uncontrollable electrical disturbances in the brain.

Memory Issues

Forgetfulness, confusion, or trouble concentrating may occur.
