How to Protect Against Brain Tumors

Healthy Diet

Maintain a balanced diet rich in vegetables, fruits, and whole grains to support brain health.

Limit Radiation Exposure

Avoid excessive exposure to mobile phones and unnecessary CT scans.

Regular Exercise

Engage in regular physical activity to boost overall and neurological health.

Manage Stress

Practice mindfulness, yoga, or meditation to reduce stress, which can impact brain function.
